Origins of Crash Games

The Crash Game Concept

Crash games emerged as a new category of online casino games characterized by:

  • A multiplier that increases from 1x over time
  • A random "crash" point that ends the round
  • Players choosing when to cash out
  • Simple, fast-paced gameplay

The crash game concept has roots in various sources:
