Important: Understanding Randomness

Each round in Skyward is completely independent of previous rounds. Past results do not influence future outcomes. The game uses a random number generator, making it impossible to predict when the balloon will pop. This is known as the gambler's fallacy - the mistaken belief that past events affect future probabilities in random events.
